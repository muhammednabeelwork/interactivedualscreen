Interactive Dual-Screen Player

Control a high-performance big screen display with a touch-based small screen interface

📱 1. Small Screen Controller

Open this interface on your touchscreen controller device. The screen will remain blank black initially. Tapping or dragging over the four invisible quadrants triggers high-speed playback command signals.

Open Controller Tab Drag to a separate secondary monitor, then press F11 to go full screen

🖥️ 2. Big Screen Display

Open this interface on the main display screen. It loops a standby logo/home video, transitioning instantly to zone videos upon touch and fading back to home after 5 seconds of inactivity.

Open Display Tab Unmute audio after opening. Press F11 for immersive kiosk presentation
